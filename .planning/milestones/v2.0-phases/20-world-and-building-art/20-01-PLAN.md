This creates a natural dirt-to-grass transition instead of a hard pixel edge. Do this in the canvas rendering pass after the grid is laid out (iterate dirt tiles, check neighbors, draw border pixels).

**2. Widen paths slightly:**

Add a small natural pond in an open grass area. Pick a location that doesn't overlap buildings or paths -- a good spot is near the bottom-center area between the two bottom buildings, offset slightly. The pond should be:
- About 5-6 tiles wide and 3-4 tiles tall (irregular oval shape)
