The function should use seeded random placement (same seededRandom pattern from tilemap-builder.ts) to place scenery sprites. Use a different seed (e.g., 7777) to avoid correlation with tilemap decoration positions.

**Placement rules:**

Define bounding boxes for all buildings (464x336 at their positions) and a campfire exclusion zone (64x64 + 20px margin). Scenery must NOT overlap these zones.

The world has four "gap" areas where scenery should concentrate:
- **Top strip:** y=0 to y=16 (above buildings) -- sparse trees along edge
- **Bottom strip:** y=752 to y=768 (below buildings) -- sparse trees along edge
- **Left strip:** x=0 to x=16 -- sparse trees along edge
- **Right strip:** x=1008 to x=1024 -- sparse trees along edge
- **Center cross:** The 64px-wide gap between buildings (horizontal and vertical strips through the center, excluding campfire zone)
- **Corner pockets:** Small triangular gaps at the world corners outside building bounds

**Scenery placement catalog:**

Trees (SCEN-01):
- Place 8-12 pine_tree sprites scattered in the gap areas, preferring edges and corners
- Place 6-8 oak_tree sprites scattered, slightly more toward center gaps
- Trees should have a slight random position offset (+/- 8px) for natural feel

Bushes and flowers (SCEN-01):
- Place 10-15 bush_large sprites near building edges and along the center cross
- Place 15-20 bush_small sprites filling gaps between trees
- Place 8-10 flowers_red clusters in visible open areas
- Place 6-8 flowers_blue clusters near the pond area (if visible) and scattered

Village props (SCEN-02):
- Place 1 well near the center (offset from campfire, perhaps at x=450, y=320 -- between campfire and Wizard Tower)
- Place 1 signpost near each path intersection with the center cross (4 total)
- Place 2-3 barrels near Tavern building edge
- Place 2-3 crates near Training Grounds building edge
- Place 1 market_stall near one building entrance

Fences (SCEN-02):
- Place fence_h segments along portions of path edges (not blocking paths, just lining them)
- Place 4-6 horizontal fence segments and 2-4 vertical fence segments
- Fences should border some paths but not fully enclose anything

Lanterns and torches (SCEN-04):
- Place 1 lantern at each path intersection with the center cross (4 total)
- Place 1-2 torches near each building entrance (8 total)
- Place 2 lanterns along the main center cross paths

All sprites should be added to the container with appropriate anchor (0.5, 1.0 for tall objects like trees/lanterns for bottom-ground placement; 0.5, 0.5 for flat objects like flowers/fences).

Sort children by Y position for basic depth ordering (trees further up appear behind trees further down).
