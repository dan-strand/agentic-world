Create `scripts/generate-characters.js` following the exact same pattern as `generate-buildings.js` (pngjs, setPixel, fillRect, drawLine helpers, PNG.sync.write).

Atlas layout: 128x384 (4 frames per row at 32px = 128px wide, 12 rows at 32px = 384px tall). Grid formula: `x = frameIndex * 32`, `y = (classIndex * 3 + stateIndex) * 32`.

Row order:
- Rows 0-2: mage (idle, walk, work)
- Rows 3-5: warrior (idle, walk, work)
- Rows 6-8: ranger (idle, walk, work)
- Rows 9-11: rogue (idle, walk, work)

Each frame is 32x32 pixels with transparent background. Initialize entire PNG buffer to transparent (alpha = 0) before drawing.

**Character class visual designs at 32x32 pixel scale:**

**MAGE (rows 0-2):**
- Silhouette: Pointed hat (tall peak ~6px above head), flowing robe body, staff in one hand
- Colors: Purple/blue robe (90, 50, 160), gold trim (200, 180, 80), staff (100, 70, 40) with glowing cyan tip (100, 200, 255)
- Skin: (255, 217, 179) for face/hands
- Idle (row 0, 4 frames): Subtle vertical bob (1-2px sinusoidal), staff tip glow flicker (alternate alpha)
- Walk (row 1, 4 frames): Robe sways side to side, alternating leg positions, staff bobs
- Work (row 2, 4 frames): Staff raised upward, casting gesture, small sparkle dots alternate positions

**WARRIOR (rows 3-5):**
- Silhouette: Helmet with visor (wider head area), plate armor body (boxy), sword at side
- Colors: Silver/gray armor (160, 160, 175), red plume on helmet (180, 40, 40), gold trim (200, 180, 80), blade silver (200, 200, 210)
- Skin: (255, 217, 179) for face slit area
- Idle (row 3, 4 frames): Subtle breathing sway, slight sword position shift
- Walk (row 4, 4 frames): Heavy stride, alternating legs wider apart, sword bobs at side
- Work (row 5, 4 frames): Sword practice -- sword position alternates between guard and strike poses

**RANGER (rows 6-8):**
- Silhouette: Hood/cowl (pointed hood shape), cloak, bow slung over shoulder, quiver on back
- Colors: Green/brown cloak (60, 100, 50), leather gear (130, 90, 50), bow wood (140, 100, 50), quiver (100, 70, 35)
- Skin: (255, 217, 179) visible under hood
- Idle (row 6, 4 frames): Slight cloak flutter (1px offset on cloak edge per frame), relaxed stance
- Walk (row 7, 4 frames): Light stride, cloak flutter more pronounced, bow bobs
- Work (row 8, 4 frames): Bow drawn/aiming pose, arrow nocked, slight pull-back variation

**ROGUE (rows 9-11):**
- Silhouette: Bandana/mask across face, light leather body (slimmer than warrior), daggers at belt
- Colors: Dark gray/black outfit (50, 50, 60), belt pouches (100, 80, 50), dagger glint (200, 200, 215), dark mask (40, 40, 50)
- Skin: (255, 217, 179) for visible eyes/forehead
- Idle (row 9, 4 frames): Subtle weight-shift side to side, dagger glint flicker
- Walk (row 10, 4 frames): Sneaky crouch-walk (slightly lower stance), light on feet
- Work (row 11, 4 frames): Lockpicking/examining gesture, hands forward with tool

**CRITICAL:** Each class must have a clearly distinct silhouette even at 32x32 scale. The hat shape (mage peaked vs warrior helmet vs ranger hood vs rogue bandana) is the primary differentiator. The body shape (mage robe vs warrior armor vs ranger cloak vs rogue slim) is the secondary differentiator.

After creating the script, run it with `node scripts/generate-characters.js` to generate the PNG.
