<objective>
Eliminate collection growth leaks, timer precision drift, and stream handle leaks for long-running stability.

Purpose: LEAK-04 (collection pruning), STAB-01 (timer modulo wrap), and STAB-02 (stream cleanup) address the remaining sources of unbounded growth. While each individual item is small, together they prevent slow memory creep over hours of operation. Timer accumulators that grow without bound will eventually lose floating-point precision (after ~10^15 ms), and unclosed streams leak file descriptors.

Output: Pruning logic for 4 collections, modulo wrap for 5 timer accumulators, finally-block stream cleanup, all with unit tests.
</objective>
